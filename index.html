CAMPUS LIFE

Campus Life

Butte Vista School is one of the 12 schools in the YCUSD district. This school teaches from Junior Kindergarten-8th Grade. This is a school where everyone is treated equally no matter what. Campus life: There is an auditotium, a playground, and many classrooms. A fun story I have to share is one day while we were doing some work, as a surprise our school mascot just came into the classroom and told us our class had won the school contest! After that, we got to have an ice cream party in class!



Achievments

Butte Vista School serves students with a variety of educational and physical needs that are integrated into age appropriate classrooms and activities. Classrooms consist of kindergarten through eighth grade general education, plus specially equipped classrooms for severely handicapped children.
